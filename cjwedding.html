C&J Wedding

The Wedding of Charlyn and Jeffrey Hitchens was a full project, as well as, my own wedding. I designed and coded a fully responsive website that is hosted on Github (View here). Guests used the website to get directions, check suggested lodging, look up our registry and rsvp for the wedding day. While designing the website I had to consider creating an experience for people of different ages and cultures. I also designed the logo, invitations, save-the-dates and 10 different icons that were used for table displays and place cards. The place cards had meal preferences on one side and seating on the other. I used MJML to create confirmation emails for the guests, as well. After several sketches, Inkscape was the main software that I used for creating designs. The main focus was to create something unique to both of us but that was understood by family and friends. The 10 icons that I created also gave an inside look into our relationship. For example, the icon for Superheroes and Wizards is a combination of our interests in Marvel films and Harry Potter.
