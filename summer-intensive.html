The Love Note Movement

Summer Intensive

I started working as the Marketing Manager at Deeply Rooted Dance Theater in early June 2018. My first assignments was to work social media marketing for the Summer Intensive. The Summer Intensive is a 4-week program for young dancers from across the country. Dancers prepare for two performances in front of an audience of over four hundred people. At a staff meeting, a new shirt design was requested that could be sold to students taking part of the program. The theme for the year was "Looking to the Youth". I didn't have much to go off besides that. I wanted to show youth coming together with past generations to learn and create. The orange represents youth and is a spring color. While the purple represents the value of the knowledge of past generations. It's like a king passing the throne to their offspring. I wanted the design to work with the typeface of the logo as well. After a sketches and different design ideas, I presented the strongest logo visually that also communicated the unity of the past and future. The staff liked the design and more were ordered to sell to the audience.
