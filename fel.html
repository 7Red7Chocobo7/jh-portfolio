Feliz Cumpleaños Char

Feliz Cumpleaños Char is a project that I worked on to celebrate my wife's 32nd birthday. We hosted friends at our apartment while I made cubanos and several sides. The idea for this project was to create a simple 2-page website to serve as a menu on the day of the celebration. It was important for the website to be a brief part of the experience. So while responsive it was only meant for viewing on tablet and mobile devices. After several sketches for the logo and layout, I began to put the designs together in Sketch. Inspiration for the colors came from the Cuban flag.
