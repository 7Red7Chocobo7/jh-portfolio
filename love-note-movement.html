The Love Note Movement

A friend of mine writes letters to family and friends as a way to keep in contact outside of using social media. She felt that letters, and even postcards, have a more personal feeling to them. Getting something in the mail, besides bills, inspires a feeling that's harder to come by these days. Like taking the plastic packaging off of a new CD. The name "The Love Note Movement" took some brainstorming together. The idea that I wanted to show with the logo was warmth and familiarity. The outline of the heart is a subtle reminder of the motivation to send the mail in the first place. After creating a few designs based on my sketches I made the final logo in Inkscape based on what I felt. The handwritten look of the typeface Black Jack was a strong last touch.
