Lituation Part 2

Lituation Part 2

The Lituation Part 2 was a fundraising event to raise money for incarnated black cis and transgender women. The event was being organized by Black Youth Project 100 (BYP100). The visuals for Lituation Part 2 were all digital and shared on social media. This design had to be created and circulated in a short period of time. There were several needs for this design. Creating a full flyer, for a Facebook event, and a version to use as a profile photo on various social media platforms. I created everything in Sketch. With little time to work, my focus was to keep the design up lifting. I also wanted to make the type stand out as part of the design since I didn't have imagery to work with. The Acherus Militant 1 typeface gave me the ability to break up the lettering into sections of different colors. The design reminded me of an African print without being too heavy handed.
