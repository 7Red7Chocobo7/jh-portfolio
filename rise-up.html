The Rise Up Project

The Rise Up Project was a logo designed for my brother. He was trying to create a Christian based sports club. While designing the logo I tried to keep in mind the strive for excellence with a team focus. I gave a organic look to the design because I felt that it was more honest to the nature of a club that centers spiritual and physical performance. After some complications my brother moved on to other pursuits. I decided to keep the logo design and change the colors from blue, grey and white and change the name.
